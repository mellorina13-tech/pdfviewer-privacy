Privacy Policy

PDF Viewer Application

Last Updated: November 14, 2025

ðŸ”’ Your Privacy Matters: This app is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal information.

1. Information Collection

PDF Light does not collect any personal information from its users. The app operates entirely on your device and does not transmit any data to external servers.

2. Data Processing

All PDF files you open and view in this application are:

  • Processed locally on your device
  • Never uploaded to any server
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Remain under your complete control

3. Storage

The app may temporarily cache PDF files in your device's local storage for performance purposes. This data:

  • Remains on your device only
  • Is not accessible to anyone else
  • Can be cleared at any time through your device settings

4. Permissions

PDF Viewer may request the following permissions:

  • Storage Access: To read and open PDF files from your device
  • Internet Access (if applicable): Only for displaying ads, not for data collection

These permissions are used solely for the app's core functionality and are never used to collect personal information.

5. Third-Party Services

This app may use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob (if applicable): For displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect device information for ad personalization. You can learn more about AdMob's privacy practices at Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Children's Privacy

This application does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app is designed for general use and does not target children specifically.

7. Data Security

Since all processing happens locally on your device and no data is transmitted to external servers, your files remain secure on your device. We recommend using your device's built-in security features to protect your data.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete on our end. All data remains on your device and under your control.
